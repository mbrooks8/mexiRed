welcome


It started with a vision and a sketch by two college students in 2004. Owners Janna Rodriguez and Oscar Torres turned that vision into what is today a bustling kitchen and restaurant in the heart of Downtown Merced. J&R Tacos opened in 2006, with one goal in mind: Create A Healthy Twist on Mexican Cuisine

Everything about J&R Tacos is authentic. From our menu to out methods to our decor. We are a locally owned independent little restaurant on Main Street with many ideas, a huge heart and a true love for our community. The food is prepared with the health conscious in mind. It’s a nod to the past with an eye on the future of cuisine in America.
